# TinkerCad Quickstart Guide

TinkerCad is a free, browser-based tool for 3D design and electronics. Here’s how to get started quickly!

## 1. Sign Up & Log In
- Go to [tinkercad.com](https://www.tinkercad.com/)
- Create a free account or log in

## 2. Start a New Design
- Click "Create new design"
- You’ll see the workplane (grid) and basic shapes

## 3. Basic Controls
- **Move:** Click and drag shapes
- **Rotate:** Use the curved arrows
- **Resize:** Drag the white handles
- **Zoom:** Scroll or use the zoom buttons
- **Pan:** Right-click and drag

## 4. Add Shapes
- Drag shapes from the sidebar onto the workplane
- Combine shapes to make new objects
- Use the "Hole" tool to cut out parts

## 5. Group & Align
- Select multiple shapes
- Click "Group" to combine
- Use "Align" for neat placement

## 6. Export for 3D Printing
- Click "Export"
- Choose .STL for 3D printers
- Download your file

## 7. Save & Share
- TinkerCad autosaves your work
- Click "Share" to send a link or invite collaborators

## Tips
- Keep designs small for fast printing
- Use the ruler for precise measurements
- Try the text tool for custom names
